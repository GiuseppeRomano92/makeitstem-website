Meet the Founder

Dr Giuseppe Romano

I'm a Chartered Engineer (CEng MIMechE, MIMMM, FHEA) with a PhD from the University of Manchester, where I developed micromechanical models for fibre-hybrid composites. I'm currently a Senior R&D Engineer at Synopsys, having previously worked as an R&D Engineer at Ansys Materials — building materials intelligence tools used across aerospace, automotive, and energy simulation workflows. I also spent over four years as NAFEMS' Italia Regional Representative, organising engineering simulation webinars and conferences.

Make IT STEM grew out of something I kept seeing — through teaching at Manchester and mentoring on the side — talented graduates who had strong technical foundations but struggled to translate that into the confidence, communication skills, and strategic thinking that employers actually look for. I started mentoring informally, then realised this gap needed a dedicated, structured approach.

Today I help students and early-career engineers bridge that gap — combining real CAE and materials R&D experience with personalised, 1:1 mentoring that goes far beyond generic careers advice.
