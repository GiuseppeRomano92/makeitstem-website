Meet the Founder

Dr Giuseppe Romano

I'm a Chartered Engineer and Senior R&D Engineer at Synopsys, with a PhD from the University of Manchester in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering. I've spent over 5 years working at the intersection of advanced materials, simulation, and product development.

Make IT STEM grew out of something I kept seeing: talented graduates who had strong technical foundations but struggled to translate that into the confidence, communication skills, and strategic thinking that employers actually look for. I started mentoring informally, then realised this gap needed a dedicated, structured approach.

Today I help students and early-career engineers bridge that gap — combining real industry insight with personalised mentoring that goes far beyond generic careers advice.
